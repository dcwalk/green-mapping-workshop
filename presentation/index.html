This workshop is part of a design research project exploring how to design online community maps to support discovery of urban agriculture and sustainability initiatives.

Workshop Plan

In 3 hours we will:

  • learn about online mapping projects
  • look at, and test, existing mapping tools
  • prototype maps and put them online!

What is a ‘Green Map’?

According to Green Map Systems, green maps are:

“locally created environmentally-themed maps...plot[ting] the locations of a community's natural, cultural and sustainable resources”

TransforMap sees green maps as highlighting:

“the initiatives, communities, projects... dedicated to... serving the common good and/ or contributing to a sustainable way of life”

How do you make a Web Map?

There are lots of tools! (overwhelming)

Some help you do everything and others only help with one small piece, most could be considered Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

“designed to capture, store, manipulate, analyze, manage, and present all types of spatial or geographical data”

A note on cost: most of these tools are free up to a point
